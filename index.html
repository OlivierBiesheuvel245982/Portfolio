BUAS DataLab Tasks

AI in Chappie (2015)

This project explores the portrayal of artificial intelligence in the movie Chappie (2015) and its relevance to real-world AI concepts. Chappie tells the story of an AI-powered robot that gains consciousness, raising questions about the nature of AI, machine learning, and sentience. The project analyzes the film’s depiction of AI within the context of modern AI technologies, discussing how close we are to achieving such advanced capabilities. Ethical concerns around AI development, autonomy, and control are also highlighted. This project provides a critical evaluation of the feasibility of AI like Chappie in today's world.
